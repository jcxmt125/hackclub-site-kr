Android help - AOSP / apps / uninstall

Let's begin.
First, make sure you'd like to uninstall the app - all data will be erased from your device.
Your photos, contacts, and settings likely won't be erased.
If you decide to reinstall the app later, you may have to sign in again, configure the app again, and more.

From your home screen, swipe up to open your app drawer.
It should show all of your installed apps.
Here, find the app you'd like to remove.
Long press the app until a dialouge pops up, and while continue holding the app, move it towards the top of the screen.
Drop the app in the section titled "uninstall".
In the confirmation prompt, tap OK.

Some apps may be unable to be removed.
You won't see the uninstall option for them.
