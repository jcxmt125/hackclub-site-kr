Android help - AOSP / disruptions / overlays

An app may "display over other apps", obscuring your view.
This can also happen on the lockscreen.
You can disable an app's ability to do this entirely.

Identify the app causing the problem.
Swipe up to open the app drawer.
Locate the app in your app, then click on the "i" icon or the "app info" button to open its app info page.
Scroll down to find "Display over other apps" under advanced.
Tap, and toggle it off.

You can follow a similar procedure to enable this again at a later date.
Simply toggle the feature back on, following the previous steps.

For especially disruptive apps, consider uninstalling them.
This feature can be used by malicious actors to steal your personal information.
