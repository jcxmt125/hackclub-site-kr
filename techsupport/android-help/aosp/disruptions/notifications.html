Android help - AOSP / disruptions / notifications

There are multiple ways of dealing with this issue.

  • Silencing the notification category
  • Disabling the notification category
  • Disabling the app's notification entirely
Each method is outlined below, with a video demonstration.

Notification category silencing

From the notification shade, long-press the offending notification.
Select "silent" to recieve similar notifications "silently".
They won't ring your device, but it'll be there in your notification shade.

Notification category disabling

From the notification shade, long-press the offending notification.
Select "turn off notifications".
A pop up will open, and the category of the notification will blink to indicate which one the notification belongs to.
Toggle the category off, then click apply.
This will cause all similar notifications to not arrive at all.

Disabling notifications for an entire app

This will prevent you from recieving any notifications from that app, no matter how important.
From the notification shade, long-press the offending notification.
Tap the cogwheel icon.
Toggle off "All [app name] notifications".
You can also turn off individual categories from here instead.
The category of the notification will blink to indicate which one the notification belongs to.
